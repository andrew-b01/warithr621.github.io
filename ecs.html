  • 2020-2021 FTC Season (12 September 2020 - 22 May 2021:

    Unfortunately since Slack does not allows scrolling over ten thousand messages due to subscription, I am at the moment unable to collect any exact data on placings that we had. However, I can definitely say that my first year participating in a Robotics club was a wonderful experience. Part of the programming team on FTC Team 12106 at Lowery Freshman Center, we met twice a week in order to discuss the many aspects going into this competition: monetary situations, building the robot, programming its various functions, and presenting our progress at competitions throughout the year.
